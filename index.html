ABOUT

Application deadline: 31st of August 2023

We are excited to present you with the orchestra conducting masterclasses with Johannes Schlaefli for young professional conductors and advanced conducting students. A maximum number of 8 participants will receive:

  • At least 120 minutes of podium time each with the St. Christopher's orchestra;
  • Daily individual and group lessons, as well as coaching in front of the orchestra;
  • Professional video recording on the last day of the masterclass;
  • A certificate of successful completion of the masterclass.
Rehearsals will take place every day from the 6th until the 10th of November at 9-13 h. Participants will have their first meeting with Prof. Johannes Schlaefli on the evening of the 5th of November.

Selected active participants will be informed via email in the middle of September 2023.

REPERTOIRE

B. Bartok: Divertimento for String Orchestra
A. Dvorak: Serenade for Strings, op. 22 (2nd and 4th mov.)
E. Elgar: Serenade for Strings, op. 20
E. Elgar: Elegy, op. 58
W. A. Mozart: Divertimento in F major, K. 138
A. Webern: Langsamer Satz

FEES

Application fee: 50 euros
Course fee (active participants): 980 euros*
Course fee (passive participants): 80 euros**

Deposit (payable until the 30th of September, 2023): 480 euros
Balance (payable until the 6th of November, 2023): 500 euros

Travel and accommodation expenses are not covered.

* The students accepted to the masterclass can apply for a grant of 500 euros, making the course fee more affordable. It will be possible to grant up to 2 people. The participants will receive more information about it privately once they receive an acceptance letter.
** Students of the Lithuanian Academy of Music and Theatre are allowed to observe the masterclasses free of charge. About your participation, please inform us in advance.
